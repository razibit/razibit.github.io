2023 Computer Vision, Healthcare

Developed a deep learning model that can detect COVID-19 from chest X-ray images with high accuracy. The model uses convolutional neural networks and was trained on a dataset of over 10,000 X-ray images. The project aims to provide a fast and reliable screening tool for healthcare providers.

Bangla Handwritten Digit Recognition

2022 Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing

Created a model that can recognize handwritten Bangla digits with high accuracy. The system was trained on the NumtaDB dataset and achieved a 98.2% accuracy rate. This project helps bridge the digital divide by supporting Bangla language recognition in digital systems.
